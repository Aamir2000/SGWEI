ABOUT
About The National Council of YMCAs of India Since its inception in 1891 it has been serving the Youth through the development of their mind, body and spirit. It recognizes the fact that the young people today are not only a major resource and inheritors of the future world but also are active contributors towards the betterment of the society.
ABOUT SIR GEORGE WILLIAMS
(11 October 1821 – 6 November 1905) Sir George Williams is the founder of the YMCA movement. He established the YMCA in London, England in 1844. The YMCA “Sir George Williams Education Institute” is yet another feather in the cap of The National YMCAs of India, a reputed and front line constituent of the World Alliance of YMCAs of 119 YMCA movements across six continents into a worldwide following of over 58 millions. It is a benign initiative to promote, encourage and equip the youth and the young through various short-term professional courses in becoming more dynamic and confident individuals and above all become employable for self sustenance.
